Fire Safety and Emergency Response

Fire Safety Tips

  • Keep all fire exits clear and accessible at all times.
  • Ensure fire extinguishers are visible and regularly maintained.
  • Store flammable materials safely, away from heat sources.
  • Never leave cooking or heating equipment unattended.

What to Do in Case of Fire

  • Activate the nearest fire alarm to alert others.
  • Evacuate immediately using the nearest safe exit.
  • If smoke is present, stay low to the ground to avoid inhalation.
  • Follow emergency exit signs to a safe assembly point.
Stay safe and always be prepared. Fire safety saves lives.
