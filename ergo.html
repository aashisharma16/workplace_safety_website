Ergonomics in the Workplace

What is Ergonomics?

Ergonomics is the science of designing workplaces, tools, and tasks to fit the physical capabilities and limitations of workers. A well-designed workspace reduces strain, prevents injuries, and enhances productivity.

Benefits of Ergonomics

  • Reduces the risk of musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs).
  • Increases employee comfort and job satisfaction.
  • Enhances productivity by minimizing fatigue and discomfort.
  • Promotes better posture and reduces physical strain.

Key Ergonomic Tips

  • Adjust your chair to support your lower back.
  • Position your computer screen at eye level to reduce neck strain.
  • Keep your keyboard and mouse at a comfortable height to prevent wrist strain.
  • Take short breaks every 30 minutes to stretch and rest your eyes.
Pro Tip: Use an ergonomic chair and consider a standing desk for alternating between sitting and standing during work hours.
